About me

I'm Jashanpreet Singh, a 20yo from Punjab, India, with big dreams. I finished high school in 2023 from APS Public School, Amritsar. I got access to the internet in 6th grade when YouTube was getting popular in India. I was super curious about what I could do online, so I started learning video editing and even picked up Photoshop. In 9th grade, I chose Computer IT as my optional subject, and thats when I got into coding. In 2023, I started a YouTube channel and hit 589 subscribers with 107K views in just three months. I was so happy, but my parents wasn't. They thought I was wasting time, so they decided to send me abroad.
